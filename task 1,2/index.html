PVR Metals & Minerals

PVR Metals & Minerals is known for providing High-Grade Ceramic Raw Materials such as Feldspar & Quartz.

Our team ensures to deliver the best product as per the customer's demands. The latest Technology embedded in our unit has the potential to meet the requirements of industrial-grade quality.
